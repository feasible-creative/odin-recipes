Chicken Parmesan

Let's Make a Classic Dish!

Ingredients:

  • 1 Pound of Boneless Chicken Breast
  • 1 Bottle of Hot Sauce
  • 1 Packed of Ranch Dressing Powder
  • 1 Stick of Unsalted Butter
  • 1 Cup of Milk

Steps:

  1. Place the chicken breasts in the bottom of a 3 quart or larger slow cooker. Cut the butter into chunks and place the butter on top of the chicken. Pour the hot sauce mixture over the chicken and butter.
  2. Place the lid on the slow cooker and cook on low heat for four hours. After four hours, use two forks to shred the chicken (if it's not tender enough to shred yet, let it cook a little longer).
  3. Serve the shredded chicken and sauce on sandwiches, in quesadillas, on top of nachos or salads, or as a dip for crackers and chips.
